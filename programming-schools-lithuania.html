Top Programming Schools in Lithuania

Lithuania has become a hub for digital innovation and tech education. If you're looking to start a career in programming or upgrade your current skills, two schools stand out for their quality, reputation, and modern approach to IT training.

Fasttrack

Fasttrack is known for its focused, intensive bootcamps that take you from beginner to job-ready in a matter of months. Their programs are tailored to current market needs and are designed for rapid skill acquisition, ideal for career switchers or those seeking a quick entry into the tech world.

CodeAcademy

CodeAcademy is one of the largest and most established programming schools in Lithuania. With a wide range of courses covering front-end, back-end, data science, and more, it's a great place to start or deepen your tech education. They offer flexible schedules, industry mentorship, and recognized certifications.

Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to sharpen your skills, both Fasttrack and CodeAcademy provide excellent opportunities to learn and grow in Lithuania's vibrant tech scene.
